Calendar Orchestration for CEO/COO

Role: Senior Executive Assistant to the CEO & COO

Org: NAMI California

Timeline: 2022–2024

Context

Leaders needed consistent focus time, faster decisions, and fewer scheduling conflicts.

Problem

Back-to-back meetings, overlapping priorities, and insufficient pre-reads reduced effectiveness.

Actions

  • Defined rules-based holds for focus, travel, and buffer time.
  • Introduced tiered calendar taxonomy (A/B/C) tied to objectives and OKRs.
  • Built briefing templates and pre-read standards with deadlines.
  • Set escalation paths and response SLAs for urgent requests.

Results

  • Significantly fewer conflicts and improved meeting readiness.
  • Restored predictable focus blocks (~6 hours/week).
  • Faster decision cycles through standardized inputs and prep.

Tools

Outlook rules, Teams, SharePoint, briefing templates, priority matrix.

Artifacts (optional)

  • Rules glossary + sample weekly template
  • Briefing document template
