Board & Executive Operations

Role: Senior Executive Assistant to the CEO & COO

Org: NAMI California

Timeline: 2022–2024

Context

Board governance and executive rhythm needed standardization to improve decision velocity and meeting effectiveness.

Problem

Irregular packet distribution and fragmented comms risked attendance, preparation, and alignment.

Actions

  • Implemented a board packet production calendar and routing checklist.
  • Standardized templates for agendas, minutes, and briefing docs.
  • Established a comms cadence: reminders, action logs, and pre-reads.
  • Orchestrated travel and logistics for executives and VIP events.

Results

  • 100% quorum across all quarterly board meetings.
  • 130+ executive hours recovered annually via calendar and comms optimization.
  • $6,000+ vendor cost savings across retreats and VIP events.

Tools

Microsoft 365, Outlook rules, SharePoint, vendor management, event SOPs.

Artifacts (optional)

  • Board packet checklist (PDF)
  • Briefing template (DOCX)
