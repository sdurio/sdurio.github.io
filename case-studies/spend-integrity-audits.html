Spend Integrity Audits

Role: Program Operations Analyst

Org: California Department of Corrections & Rehabilitation (CDCR)

Timeline: 2015–2021

Context

High-volume purchasing activity required proactive controls to minimize financial misallocations and compliance risk.

Problem

Manual reconciliations struggled to catch high-risk errors quickly.

Actions

  • Built audit queries and exception reports targeting known risk patterns.
  • Implemented periodic reviews and escalation paths.
  • Partnered with finance to remediate and prevent repeat issues.

Results

  • Identified and corrected $75k+ in errors annually.
  • Improved compliance posture and data accuracy.

Tools

Excel/Access analytics, exception reporting, SOPs, RACI.
