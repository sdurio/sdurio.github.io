Program Transparency Platform

Role: Executive & Board Operations Associate

Org: Umoja Community Education Foundation

Timeline: 2021–2022

Context

Leadership needed better visibility into tasks, blockers, and deadlines across programs.

Problem

Fragmented tracking and limited status reporting contributed to project slippage.

Actions

  • Implemented a cloud-based project management platform with shared dashboards.
  • Standardized status updates and artifact requirements for each workstream.
  • Defined ownership (RACI) and handoffs to reduce bottlenecks.

Results

  • Increased task transparency and accountability.
  • Reduced project delays by 35%.
  • Supported full funding across active programs via better reporting.

Tools

Project management platform (cloud), shared dashboards, RACI, SOPs.
