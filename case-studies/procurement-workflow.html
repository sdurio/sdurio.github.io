Procurement Workflow Revamp

Role: Program Operations Analyst

Org: California Department of Corrections & Rehabilitation (CDCR)

Timeline: 2015–2021

Context

Procurement and contracting processes needed clearer intake, faster routing, and consistent documentation to support equity and speed.

Problem

Fragmented intake and manual steps increased cycle time and error risk.

Actions

  • Rebuilt intake → PO → invoice flow with standardized forms and SLAs.
  • Created documentation standards and routing checklists.
  • Implemented a small-business discount calculator to improve equity in awards.

Results

  • Contract processing time reduced by 30%.
  • More equitable vendor awards through transparent discounting.

Tools

Process maps, intake forms, Excel-based calculator, policy alignment.
