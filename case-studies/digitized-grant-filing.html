Digitized Grant Filing System

Role: Grant Program Administrative Coordinator

Context: Statewide grantee reporting and audits required faster, cleaner documentation.

Problem

Paper-heavy processes and inconsistent filing slowed audits and compliance checks.

Actions

  • Designed audit-ready spreadsheets + standardized folder/filename taxonomy.
  • Migrated records to a centralized, searchable digital system.
  • Trained staff; created SOP with screenshots and RACI.

Results

  • Administrative processing time ↓ 40%.
  • Audit preparation time ↓ 35%.

Tools

Microsoft 365 (Excel/SharePoint), governance templates, SOP playbook.

  • (Optional) Redacted spreadsheet template
  • (Optional) Filing SOP (PDF)
