Digitized Grant Filing System

Role: Grant Program Administrative Coordinator

Org: Judicial Council of California

Timeline: 2025

Context

Statewide grantee reporting and legislative audits required faster, cleaner documentation across multiple programs.

Problem

Paper-heavy and inconsistent filing slowed audit prep and created risk of missing artifacts.

Actions

  • Designed audit-ready spreadsheet templates with validation and metadata.
  • Standardized folder structure and filename taxonomy for statewide use.
  • Migrated records into a centralized, searchable digital system.
  • Created SOP with screenshots, RACI, and quality checks; trained staff.

Results

  • Administrative processing time decreased by 40%.
  • Audit preparation time decreased by 35%.
  • Improved compliance readiness and reporting accuracy for 15+ MOUs.

Tools

Microsoft 365 (Excel, SharePoint), governance templates, SOP playbook.
